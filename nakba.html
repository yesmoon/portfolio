The Ethnic Cleansing of Palestine

Infographic Design

This timeline is based on Ilan Pappe's book titled The Ethnic Cleansing of Palestine. The timeline starts in 1878 with the first Zionist colony in Palestine, and ends with a series of events spanning 1948-1949.

Please not that this timeline does not cover 100% of the facts, and that politics do not define the residents of either country.
