Pancakes on 26th

A Book About a Block in San Francisco

The first time I met a friend of mine, we had pancakes on 26th street at Chloe’s Café.

For a semester-long project, I visited 26th St. in San Francisco twice a week. The content is based on my time spent in the block between Church and Dolores street. Most of my visits had prompts—take 50 photos during one visit, collect trash, and invite people to join you.
